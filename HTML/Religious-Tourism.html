Egypt is rich in religious monuments: historical temples, synagogues, churches, and mosques. Religious tourists come to perform rituals, visit spiritual places, and cemeteries of the divines. Coptic monuments and churches are spread all over the country, they are usually related to the Journey of the Holy Family. Muslim and Christian tourists come from all over the world to see there religious monuments, Like : Al-Azhar Mosque and Saint-Catrene Cherch .
